# Outreach Research Notes — Nursing Home Tracker

## Key Academic Papers (cite when reaching out to researchers)

1. **Gupta et al. (2021)** — "Does Private Equity Investment in Healthcare Benefit Patients? Evidence from Nursing Homes" (NBER #28474)
   - Found PE ownership → 10% higher short-term mortality, higher per-patient Medicare costs
   - Used CMS data manually classified — our tool automates this

2. **Gandhi et al. (2023)** — "Private Equity and the Provision of Health Care Services" (Management Science)
   - Similar finding: PE ownership associated with lower quality metrics
   - Used LTC Focus dataset + manual ownership classification

3. **Grabowski et al. (2020)** — "Nursing Home Ownership and Quality" (various papers in JAMA, NEJM)
   - Long-running research program on ownership → quality linkage
   - Our tool provides the automated infrastructure for this research

## Industry Context

- **Nov 2023 CMS Rule:** Final rule requiring disclosure of PE ownership in nursing homes
  - Creates timely hook for outreach: "Our tool helps implement the new transparency requirements"
  - Rule takes effect 2024, so data is becoming available

- **Market Size:** 15,600 nursing homes, ~1.3M residents
  - ~70% for-profit, ~25% nonprofit, ~5% government
  - PE ownership estimated at 5-10% of facilities but growing rapidly

- **Competitors:**
  - ProPublica Nursing Home Inspect — good UI but no ownership analysis, no penalty cross-linking
  - Definitive Healthcare — enterprise pricing ($100K+), no public access
  - StarPRO — financial focus, expensive
  - CMS Care Compare — raw data, no analysis

## Outreach Timing
- Best to reach out after pipeline data confirms real insights
- Lead with specific findings (e.g., "PE-owned facilities average X stars")
- Academic outreach: timing around conference deadlines (AcademyHealth June, NBER Summer)
- Media outreach: tie to CMS transparency rule implementation
